## Joaquín Bravo

Creator, Writer, Director | Uruguay  

Audio-visual creator specialised in script writing, direction, montage and animation. The preference for comedy and a pursuit for creativity are his trademarks. Joaquín studied to become a bachelor in Visual Communication Design in the University of the Republic (UdelaR), he studied Image and Sound Design in the University of Buenos Aires (UBA) and in Dodecá film school. He has also taken part in numerous scriptwriting workshops within Uruguay and Argentina. He has been working in the audio-visual industry since 2011.
