Ludmila Wagnest is an Argentinian Writer, Director and Producer based in Madrid, working across fiction, television and branded content. She is the Creator and Director of the series Black Sunday (Prime Video), an Argentina–Spain co-production recognized at international festivals, and Co-Creator of the award-winning web series Prohibido Silbar. Her work blends a strong visual identity with emotionally driven storytelling, exploring themes of identity and contemporary social experiences. Alongside her narrative projects, she develops audiovisual content for Warner Music Spain and has collaborated on television productions such as Tierra de Sabores for Canal Sur. As Co-Founder of Quiet Producciones, she focuses on author-driven cinema, moving fluidly between writing, directing and creative producing while shaping projects from concept to final delivery.
