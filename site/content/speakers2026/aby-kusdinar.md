Aby Kusdinar began his journey in Indonesian cinema in 2017 as an online film reviewer on the YouTube channel Sumatran Bigfoot. His films blend multiple genres and often serve as metaphors for subjects that are difficult to address openly within his community. His work explores themes such as sexuality, personal rebellion, and the double standards of Islamic patriarchy that suppress individual freedom.
