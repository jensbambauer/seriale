Kyojun Kim is a Seoul-based Producer, Bilingual Announcer, and Digital Media Strategist operating at the intersection of technology and global entertainment. Currently pursuing a Master’s in Management Information Systems at Seoul National University, he previously earned an MBA and degrees in Psychology and Communication. Kyojun has built an extensive portfolio managing social media operations for premier entities, including MLB Korea and Tottenham Hotspur F.C. Korea. His leadership in the international circuit is distinguished by his roles as an Organizer and Host for the Yeosu International Webfest 2024 and the Yeosu International Web Drama Film Festival 2026. With professional roots as a Creative Director and a former Producer at IHQ Media, Kyojun blends academic rigor with hands-on expertise in content planning and high-end technical production. He is dedicated to evolving the landscape of independent digital storytelling through cross-cultural collaboration.
