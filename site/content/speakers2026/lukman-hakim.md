He serves as Cultural Programme Coordinator at the Goethe-Institut Bandung, managing festivals and interdisciplinary cultural programmes. His work spans film, music, visual arts, performance, design, and the wider creative economy. He is the Festival Manager of Seriale Indonesia, focusing on developing series-based initiatives, fostering international collaboration, and strengthening networks between filmmakers, creative communities, and cultural institutions.
