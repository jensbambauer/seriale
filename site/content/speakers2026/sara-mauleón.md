Sara Mauleón is a Spanish Audiovisual Producer, Musician, and Expert in cultural communication with over a decade of experience in the creative industries. As Founder and CEO of MAULEÓN, she supports artists, creators, and cultural projects in developing authentic narratives and digital strategies. Her work includes the internationally nominated series About:/thenew and the collective feature film Begirada, where she wrote, directed, and produced her piece Muga. She works internationally as a Mentor and Speaker and has served as a Jury member at festivals including the San Sebastián International Film Festival, Rio Web Fest, and die Seriale. Sara is also a Guitarist for the alternative metal band Delenda Est and currently serves as President of Hemen, the Basque Association of Women in the Audiovisual Industry.
