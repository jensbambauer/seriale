Christian Stadach is a Director, Producer, and Storyteller with a background in film, advertising, and visual effects. He has led hundreds of projects for brands, agencies, and original productions, combining cinematic craft with a strong interest in emerging technologies such as AI, XR, and interactive storytelling. Before co-founding Tag & Nacht Media, he worked in high-end postproduction, including compositing at Pixomondo on major international productions. Alongside his commercial and narrative work, he also teaches at university level, with a focus on storytelling, immersive media, and creative direction. His work is driven by the belief that strong ideas, emotional clarity, and technical precision create lasting impact.
