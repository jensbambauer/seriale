Founded two decades ago, HG Distribution Inc. has grown into an internationally respected boutique distributor, guiding independent producers from concept to global audiences. Over the last eight years the company has focused on the evolving landscape of digital rights and television technologies, adapting its expertise to emerging platforms while preserving a commitment to distinctive programming. HG’s catalogue spans children’s content, documentaries, series and feature films, and its hands-on, client-centered approach, rooted in honesty and integrity, has earned lasting industry relationships. Known for attentive after-sales support and a warm, personal touch, HG Distribution will be celebrating its 20th anniversary this year.
