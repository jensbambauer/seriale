Suzette Laqua is an Executive Producer, Writer, Speaker, and Entrepreneur based in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. With her innovative vision and achievements, Suzette has played a significant role in shaping the global digital storytelling landscape. In addition to serving as a Board Member of WebSeries Canada, Suzette is currently a Co-Producer on the feature film Werewolf and serves as Executive Producer on an American documentary being filmed by a Canadian documentary production company, reflecting her growing involvement in international productions and cross-border collaborations. She has also served and continues to serve as a Jury Member for festivals and contributes as a Panelist and Speaker at many events and festivals around the world. A respected leader in the entertainment industry, Suzette’s career is defined by her passion for the arts, dedication to innovation, and ability to inspire meaningful conversations about the future of storytelling.
